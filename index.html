The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

reating a nationwide digital ecosystem to

Green Energy

a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support
economic growth and global competitiveness.

Dear Citizens,

Bangladesh 2.0 is our future—a nation of innovation, equality, and sustainability. Together, we will empower our youth, modernize industries, and protect our environment. This is our chance to rise as a united, progressive nation that leads by example.Let us embrace change with determination and pride. The world is watching, and it’s time for Bangladesh to shine.


Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Cheif Advisor of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
